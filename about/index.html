About

Hey everyone! I’m Peyton Walters and I’m a student at the University of Pennsylvania studying Networked and Social Systems Engineering.

I’m particularly interested in IP networking, distributed systems, and Unix-based operating systems, but there are far more things than that which pique my interest. Some of my past and current work includes:

  • Caplance, a network-level load balancer in the spirit of Maglev
  • Jiffy, a program that automated the deployment of KVM/Libvirt virtual machines
  • An ansible and ruby program to automate the deployment of ssh keys
  • Go Log Server, simple program to send rsyslog entries to a redis stream
  • Alpine, a chef cookbook to add chef support to Alpine Linux
